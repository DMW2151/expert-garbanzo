Instructions

Click the buttons above to toggle the layers displayed on the map. You can hover over elements on the map for more detail

Live

The live layer displays the current position of any bus, train, or tram in the HSL metro area. Clicking on a vehicle in this layer will render the past 2 hours of the vehicle's position and delay history. This layer is accurate to within 5 seconds of real-time.

Neighborhood

The neighborhood layer divides the metro area into neighborhoods and renders them as shapes on the map, the coloring of the map represents the average speed of vehicles in area in the previous 60 minutes.

GTFS

The GTFS layer displays the reported stop locations and routes in the metro system.
